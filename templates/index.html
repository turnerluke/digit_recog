About the app

This app can recognize multiple handwritten digits. Draw a digit on the canvas above and press the "Recognize" button to see a prediction. Press the "Clear" button to clear the canvas and draw a digit again. You can draw several digits. You can even try drawing non-digits, the model will try to renognize them.

Collecting the data

All drawn images are saved and will be used to improve the quality of the model.
